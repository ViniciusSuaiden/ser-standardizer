## EmoUERJ (guide)

EmoUERJ is an open dataset managed automatically by the audb library.

- **Official Site:** [audEERING datasets - EmoUERJ](https://audeering.github.io/datasets/datasets/emouerj.html)
- **License:** Creative Commons Attribution Share Alike 4.0 (open for research and commercial use with attribution).

You do not need to download anything — just run the script with `ser-std` without `input_dir`.
